### music_listening_frequency

**Original question:** How often do you listen to music? (Single selection)

**Coding:**

- 0 = Every day
- 1 = 2-3 times a week
- 2 = Once a week
- 3 = 2-3 times a month
- 4 = Once a month
- 5 = I don't listen to music

### music_listening_duration

**Original question:** How long do you typically listen to music per session? (Single selection)

**Coding:**

- 0 = Less than 10 minutes
- 1 = 10 to 30 minutes
- 2 = 30 minutes to 1 hour
- 3 = 1 hour or more
